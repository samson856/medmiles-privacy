MedMiles Privacy Policy

Tax Tracking for Medical Professionals

Effective Date: March 27, 2026

MedMiles ("the App") is a tax tracking application designed for 1099 independent contractor medical professionals. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use MedMiles. Your privacy matters to us, and we are committed to being transparent about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Password (securely hashed; we never store plaintext passwords)

Profile Information

To provide tax tracking features, you may provide:

  • Name and professional details
  • Home address (for home office deduction calculations)
  • Tax filing status and related preferences

Financial and Tax Data

The App allows you to track the following categories of data:

  • Trips and Mileage: Start and end locations, distances, dates, and purposes of travel
  • Income: Earnings records from your 1099 contract work
  • Meals: Business meal expenses including amounts and details
  • Monthly Expenses: Recurring business-related expenses
  • Miscellaneous Expenses: Other deductible costs
  • Credentials: Professional credential information relevant to your work
  • Home Office: Home office dimensions and related deduction information

Location Data

When you log a trip, the App uses the Google Maps Distance Matrix API to calculate the distance between your start and end locations. The App does not continuously track your location in the background.

Subscription Information

If you subscribe to MedMiles Pro, Apple processes your payment through StoreKit. We receive confirmation of your subscription status but do not collect or store your payment method details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your MedMiles account
  • Calculate mileage deductions using trip start and end locations
  • Track and organize your income, expenses, and deductions for tax purposes
  • Provide current IRS tax rates and standard deduction amounts
  • Manage your subscription status (Free or Pro tier)
  • Respond to support inquiries

We do not:

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising purposes
  • Use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs
  • Share your data with data brokers

3. Data Storage and Security

Your account and tax data are stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted by Supabase. We use the following security measures to protect your information:

  • Row Level Security (RLS): Database-level policies ensure that you can only access your own data. No other user can view, modify, or delete your records.
  • Encrypted Connections: All data transmitted between the App and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • Secure Authentication: Supabase Auth handles account authentication with industry-standard password hashing.

While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your MedMiles account.

4. Third-Party Services

Supabase

We use Supabase as our backend infrastructure provider for authentication and database services. Your data is stored on Supabase's secure cloud infrastructure. Supabase processes your data on our behalf and in accordance with their privacy policy.

Google Maps Distance Matrix API

When you log a trip, the App sends your trip's start and end addresses to the Google Maps Distance Matrix API to calculate the driving distance. Google may process this location data in accordance with their privacy policy. No personally identifiable information beyond the addresses is sent with these requests.

Apple StoreKit

In-app subscriptions are processed by Apple through StoreKit 2. All payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not receive or store your credit card number or payment details. For more information, see Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. Local Storage

Receipt photos are stored exclusively on your device. When you attach a photo to an expense or meal record, that image is saved in the App's local storage on your iPhone. Receipt photos are never uploaded to our servers or any cloud service.

If you delete the App or clear its data, your locally stored receipt photos will be permanently removed from your device. We recommend exporting or backing up any important receipt images before deleting the App.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access and Export

You can view all of the data you have entered in the App at any time. If you need a copy of your data, you may contact us and we will provide it in a reasonable format.

Correction

You can update or correct your information directly within the App at any time.

Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email address below. Upon receiving a verified deletion request, we will permanently remove your account and all related data from our servers within 30 days. Note that locally stored receipt photos must be deleted separately from your device.

Data Portability

You have the right to request your data in a portable format. Contact us to make this request.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at medmilesfeedback@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

MedMiles is designed for adult medical professionals and is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information promptly. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us at medmilesfeedback@gmail.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. When we make changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
